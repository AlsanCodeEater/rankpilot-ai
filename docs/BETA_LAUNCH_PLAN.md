# RankPilot AI Beta Launch Plan

## 1. Beta Goal
Validate the core value proposition of RankPilot AI: saving merchants time on SEO optimization while tangibly improving product discovery and conversion rates. We aim to identify edge cases, refine the AI prompts based on real-world product data, and ensure application stability under varying store sizes.

## 2. Target Merchant Types
- **Store Size**: Small to medium (50 - 5,000 products).
- **Niche**: E-commerce stores with frequently changing inventory (e.g., apparel, electronics) or highly competitive niches requiring constant SEO tuning.
- **Pain Point**: Merchants lacking dedicated SEO staff or struggling with organic traffic.

## 3. Beta Onboarding Checklist
- [ ] Send personalized beta invite email.
- [ ] Provide the beta installation link (Custom App link).
- [ ] Share the `BETA_TEST_SCRIPT.md` with the merchant.
- [ ] Schedule a 15-minute onboarding call (optional, but recommended for early testers).
- [ ] Set expectations: App is in beta; bug reports are highly appreciated.

## 4. Beta Test Tasks (For Merchants)
- Connect store and sync products.
- Run an AI audit on a subset of products.
- Review and apply at least 5 AI suggestions.
- Activate the Web Pixel for ROI tracking.
- Monitor the Discovery Analytics dashboard over 7 days.

## 5. Feedback Questions
- How intuitive was the process of reviewing and applying AI suggestions?
- Did the AI suggestions accurately reflect your brand's voice and intent?
- Did you experience any noticeable performance issues during product sync or bulk audits?
- Is the Discovery Analytics dashboard providing actionable insights for your store?
- What one feature would you add to make this app indispensable?

## 6. Known Limitations (To communicate)
- The Web Pixel requires the merchant to approve the `write_pixels` scope and uses a custom endpoint.
- AI generation speed is dependent on the upstream provider (e.g., ZAI/OpenAI) and may take longer during peak times.
- Bulk applying suggestions is currently rate-limited to respect Shopify API limits.

## 7. Support Process
- Dedicated email channel: `beta-support@rankpilot.ai`
- 24-hour SLA for beta participants.
- Critical bugs (e.g., store crashes) to be hotfixed within 4 hours.
